About Us

Slush Asia

On May 13-14, Slush Asia brings together people with big dreams and determination from all over the world. Originally from Finland, Slush has grown to become one of the world’s largest startup events and has made a second home in Asia. The goal of the event is to build a startup ecosystem that supports future leaders across the globe and to ignite an entrepreneurial spirit in Asian youth through talks by experienced entrepreneurs and the game changers.

Slush Asia is the main supporter of Junction Asia as a gateway for engineers in Asia to think abroad and become a gateway from the World to Asia.

Terrada Soko

Inspired by the treasure house of Shosoin, a registered World Heritage site, as the builder and the keeper of your personal storage room, we strive to be a professional in creating spaces. We believe that space does not equate to emptiness. We believe that space embraces people’s feeling and nurtures something new. We believe that by creating space, we present new value and broader possibility. We believe that with our experience and knowledge, we see essence of things we store and give new values to them. We believe the space nurtures heritage to be passed on to the next generation and visions for the future.


Mentoring Partners

Fab Cafe

FabCafe is a space where people can come together, and connect with each other. By providing a variety of digital fabrication tools, including laser cutters and 3D printers, visitors are able to bring their digital data to life.
FabCafe is where people can enjoy the thrill of making new things together and discovering their answer to the telltale FabCafe question that we always ask, “What do you Fab?” Since the first FabCafe opened in Shibuya, Tokyo in March 2012, FabCafe network has expanded to Taipei, Barcelona, Bangkok, Toulouse and Hida.

Reaktor

Reaktor is a digital innovation studio that constructs exceptional digital services. An innovation consultant and creative software development company, Reaktor works with our client companies to bring the latest developments and best practices in the intersection of design, technology and business.

We run a 90-day innovation program with our partners to continuously build new products and business models. Some of Reaktor’s clients include Panasonic, HBO, Michael Kors, Samsung and Nokia. Reaktor has offices in New York, Helsinki and Tokyo.
