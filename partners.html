Partners

OP Lounge

Need a break from coding? Want to brainstorm with your team? The OP Lounge will be the perfect place to relax and kick back during Junction. It will be open throughout the weekend for anyone in the need of comfort.

The OP Lounge features Fatboys, couches, coffee, and snacks. It is located in the second floor of the venue with a great view of the whole Hacking Area. Turn your creative chill mode on and come enjoy the perks at the OP Lounge, the OP and Junction crews warmly welcome you!

Omena Hotels

Need a creative rest period or a good night’s sleep? Are you too jet lagged to go on nonstop for the whole 48 hours? If yes, no worries, we got you covered!

Omena Hotels and Junction provide accommodation for all the Hackers coming out of town free of charge in downtown Helsinki. Teams of 4 people will have their own rooms and smaller or larger teams will share the room with other teams. Hackers receive their keys at the opening ceremony on Friday!

Enoro

Enoro is a leading Nordic software provider for energy utilities. Customers across Europe use our software to enhance their operations, maximize the benefits of smart metering and smart grids, and stay up-to-date with regulatory compliance. We work in a growing industry, and already more than 15 000 000 energy consumers’ data across Europe is managed with our solutions. We innovate together with forward-thinking utilities to release the full potential of energy markets for our customers – today and in the future.

BaseN

With my.basen, developers can trial their concept devices, services and spimes globally in a matter of hours. my.basen enables you to invent an Uber, AirBnB or Nest Thermostat - every day.

Developers taking part in the workshop will see how my.basen works and how quickly new applications can be developed with it. After the workshop the participants are invited to get their free my.basen accounts to start creating their own massively scalable, fault tolerant, secure & globally available Internet-of-Things applications.

Cozify

The Cozify Hub connects different devices from different manufacturers into one seamlessly working entity. Cozify uses open standards and technologies - And we want to be an open platform ourselves. To embrace the open approach, We will setup a developer forum and provide open APIs for all.

You will get an API to create your own rules for the hub. For example, “Blink lights when someone is at the door” or “Wake me up with music, lights and fresh coffee”. Later on, we will let you integrate your own devices and cloud services with Cozify platform.
