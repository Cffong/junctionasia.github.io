Overview

We will never release your personal details to any unassociated 3rd party entities, nor will it sell your personal details for profit to any 3rd party entity.

Any information you provide will be used for the purpose of processing your application to enter Junction. This may include sharing content submitted by you to our official partners and affiliates.
