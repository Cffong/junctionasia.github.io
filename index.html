May 6-8. 2016. Tokyo, Japan

About

Hackathons are all about taking something you love, combining it with tech and learning something in the process. Junction brings together hackers, designers, the best partners and hardware for one weekend of hacking. Charge your laptops and bring your friends for an amazing event. Let's hack!

The best teams will get to pitch their hacks on stage at the Slush conference on the 11th of November in front of a panel of investors. The winning team will take home a 20 000€ main prize.

For everyone

No matter whether you are just a beginner or a pro, if you have the passion and a will to create something, we want you to be part of Junction!

Learn from the best

We've put together everything you need for you to build your next project. Our amazing partners will help you build whatever you want to create.

Work as a team

Get to know the best people. Get to work with other motivated individuals. We're organizing Junction to create a community of hackers and makers.

150 participants

We are selecting 150 participants for Junction Asia. Applications are open to everyone! Grab your spot before it's too late.

The best part: Attending is completly free of charge!
