Pre-emptive Healthcare and Advanced Diagnostics

For whom?

If welcomes all developers, physicians and digital health enthusiasts to co-create both:
- pre-emptive healthcare services to support healthier life and/or
- advanced diagnostics services supporting a smooth recovery when something happens
If is the leading Nordic insurance company and committed to building a safer and healthier society. In order reach the goal, If actively seeks partnerships and collaboration with the start-up community.

Track content

During the event, participants will be creating new innovative health care products and services using relevant data, the leading API’s and devices, e.g. Apple Watch. The track is powered by IBM Bluemix. Bluemix is the leading platform-as-a-service solution and brings connectivity to IBM’s Watson, which in turn has, among others, multiple health related applications.

How to win?

Be creative with the tools given, help society and win!

We’ll organize a matchmaking and introduction event  in September-October, so stay posted.

Apply and let’s enjoy a great weekend together! #ifhackathon, #ifvakuutus

FutuLabs

Create a device-based service in a weekend.

FutuLabs is the Futurice skunkworks initiative where we hack away on the latest and greatest in gadgets and technology. Our library includes devices like the Oculus Rift, Microsoft Band and various 3D printers. We’re constantly on the lookout for upcoming tech and make sure we get our hands on it as soon as possible.

What FutuLabs is for:

Hacker Happiness
FutuLabs stems from our software craftsmen honing their craft by playing with the coolest new gear. It allows our hackers to maintain and evolve their skills and enthusiasm, which is vital for creative thinking and idea generation. We don’t limit ourselves to consumer devices, but actively seek out and try to gain access to R&D projects that may someday become products. 

Designer Delight
The future is now and we want to design services for it. FutuLabs enables our designers to create experimental services for everyday things of the future. Designers can be hackers too.

Client Creativity
We want to provide our clients with the opportunity to be creative and see their business in new ways. Providing them with the latest upcoming technologies creates real life use cases for cutting edge technology.

How to win this track:
Show us a prototype of a service with great impact and a good story.

Know about some interesting gadgets that you’d want to hack on in this track? Tweet #futulabs @futurice and we’ll make sure to check it out.

The Future of Digital Payments in the Cashless Society

Paying should not be the painful part of buying. We are discovering things we love everyday and when it comes to buying them it should be a pleasant journey. As society goes cashless, how should payment be quick and easy. Help us innovate and solve actual problems and add real value to people's everyday lives by making buying simpler and safer than you can imagine. Help us identify and remove the small but annoying transnational thresholds of living, weather it be when consuming digital content, paying for downtown parking, micro donating to charities or whichever area you find needs innovation to make life better.

Klarna was founded in Stockholm in 2005 with the idea to simplify buying. We make buying simple and safe, especially on mobile, for more than 35 million users. That’s how we increase sales for more than 50 000 retailers in 18 markets. More than 1200 people work at Klarna to achieve our goal of becoming the world’s favorite way to buy.

The Future of Commerce. Built by You.

Beacons, Apple Pay, Pinterest Buy - the world of commerce is changing. Both online and off, the technologies that are available to SMBs are bringing the power that was once only available to large retailers, to everyone. Join us at Junction and help bring over 165,000 of our merchants into that future.

Shopify is a leading cloud-based commerce platform designed for small and medium-sized businesses. Merchants can use the software to design, set up and manage their stores across multiple sales channels, including web, mobile, social media, brick-and-mortar locations, and pop-up shops. Shopify currently powers over 165,000 businesses in approximately 150 countries, including: Tesla Motors, Budweiser, Google, Wikipedia, LA Lakers, GoldieBlox, and many more.

Come imagine, develop and build the future of commerce with us.

API’s

Shopify is committed to an open platform where third-parties can build apps that seamlessly integrate into the merchant experience. The possibilities are (almost) endless. In the next few months, we will also be releasing new tools to expand these possibilities.

Get started by checking out our API docs and featured SDKs.

How to win this track:

Show us something that brings simple function and high value to merchants that enables them to do something they never thought possible.

Improving Cities

Create an App using the Uber API

One of our core principals at Uber is to improve cities, which is why we are looking for developers too do just that. With the Uber API, developers have the ability to build applications that incorporate the entire Uber experience. In this track developers are challenged to use the Uber API to build an app to "improve the city". Developers may use any other APIs in the mix, but must include the Uber API as the primary use case.

How to win this track:

Use the Uber API to build the best app with the most impact. Our team focuses on the following criteria:

  1. Design
  2. Effective use of the API
  3. Originality
  4. Impact and utility
